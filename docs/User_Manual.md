## 1. Introduction
BusGo is a multi-platform system designed to streamline public transport. By leveraging real-time GPS tracking, AI-powered predictive models (Neo Engine), and seamless QR-based transactions, BusGo ensures a reliable and efficient travel experience.

## 2. Passenger Guide (BusGo Client)
The BusGo Client app is your gateway to hassle-free travel.

### 2.1 Getting Started
1. **Installation:** Install the `busgo_client` Flutter application.
2. **Registration:** Create an account using your email and phone number.
3. **Login:** Use your credentials to access the dashboard.

> **[IMAGE: Passenger App Login Screen]**

### 2.2 Finding Your Bus
- **Search:** Enter your destination in the search bar to find available routes (e.g., 138, 163).
- **Nearby Stops:** View the closest bus stops based on your current location.
- **Live Map:** Track buses in real-time and view their estimated time of arrival (ETA).

> **[IMAGE: Live Map with Bus Locations]**

### 2.3 Managing Your Trip
1. **Boarding:** Select "Start Trip" when you board the bus.
2. **Crowd Level:** Check the bus's crowd status (Low, Moderate, High) before boarding.
3. **Alighting:** Select "Alight Trip" when you reach your destination.

### 2.4 QR Card & Payments
- Access your unique **QR Card** from the profile menu.
- Present this QR code to the conductor upon exiting to finalize your fare and trip.

> **[IMAGE: Passenger QR Card Display]**
